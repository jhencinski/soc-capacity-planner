How this is modeled

Each crew is converted from its local days/hours to UTC using a fixed UTC offset, not a real calendar timezone — so daylight saving time isn't modeled. Nudge the offset by an hour when clocks change if that matters for your plan.

Panama schedule crews are modeled as steady-state coverage: every day of the week, a 12-hour day or night window. That's the actual point of a 2-2-3 rotation — the number of analysts on duty stays constant every day even though which specific people are working rotates underneath it. Enter the headcount you have on duty during a shift, not your total rotation pool.

4×10 crews are a fixed weekly pattern, not a rotation — a crew scheduled Sun–Wed is genuinely off Thu–Sat every week. Pair a Sun–Wed crew with a Wed–Sat crew to cover all 7 days.
