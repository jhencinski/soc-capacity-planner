How this is calculated

Monthly capacity = analysts × hours/day × working days/month × productivity factor.

Monthly workload = the sum of (volume × average handle time) across alert triage, investigations, and incident response, converted to hours.

Utilization = monthly workload ÷ monthly capacity.

Model adapted from Capacity Builders' analyst capacity modeling framework. The utilization bands above (under / healthy / near / over / significantly over capacity) are general guidance, not a fixed rule — tune them to your own SLAs and risk tolerance.
